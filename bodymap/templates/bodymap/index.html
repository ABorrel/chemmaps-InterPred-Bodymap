Tox21BodyMap

Tox21BodyMap provides a visualization of mapping 971 Tox21/ToxCast assay targets to regions of the human body, and allows users to identify target tissues where a specific chemical is more likely to produce effects. The webserver relies upon data on 9,270 chemicals with Tox21 assay activities, information on biological targets of the assays, and an approach that combines expert knowledge with tissue-specific gene expression levels to map assay targets to organ systems, providing an intuitive display of chemical bioactivity patterns.

Datasets

Navigation is available for the Tox21 chemical library. The list of chemicals can be browsed on the EPA chemical dashboard and on the webserver by CASRN or name. Gene expressions by body part were extracted using the Illumina Correlation Engine (version 12-2019) and Tox21 assay results were obtained from the EPA ToxCast data, invitroDBv3.2.
